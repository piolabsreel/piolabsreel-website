AGITATED

Artist: Joey X

Shot & Directed By: Piolabs Reel

Location: FCT, Nigeria

Joey X is not here to play with you. Coming out of the Federal Capital, Joey X has consistently been grinding in the underground scene putting together hits after hits. In this piece, he showscases some of his vast talents. From mixing and mastering his own beats to producing his tracks. A definite diamond in the rough.
