EXCESSIVE

Artist: King Tutt

Directed By: Piolabs Reel | Hawk Eye

Location: Ottawa

Outspoken, unruly, brash and unconventional. that's how King Tutt explained his vision for this masterpiece. Safe to say once we all aligned ourselves to the artists vision, everything else fell into place. What captivated us all was the story we could create from the tune. Well that, and all the aerial videography because lets face it, flying thousands of dollars worth of equipment over the Canal is no easy feat (trust us).
